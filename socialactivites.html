SOCIAL ACTIVITIES

Cub Scouts

Family oriented activities that build character and confidence for boys of grades 1-5. Pack meetings are held every 3rd Thursday of the month during the School Year from 7:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. at the Whitemarsh Elementary School, on Joshua Road, in Lafayette Hill.

Contact: Bill McMenamin 610.825.0282 E-mail: wmcmenamin@yahoo.com

C.Y.O.

This organization enables the Parish and High School to draw young people to participate in the life, mission and work of the faith community by fostering personal and spiritual growth of youth through the total youth ministry, through (i.e.) Liturgy, Sports, Cultural, Social and Service activities. Adult participation is needed and welcomed.

Contact: Mark Gannon 610-941-6715.

Girl Scouts

Family oriented activities that build character and confidence for boys of grades 1-5. Pack meetings are held every 3rd Thursday of the month during the School Year from 7:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. at the Whitemarsh Elementary School, on Joshua Road, in Lafayette Hill.

Contact: Bill McMenamin 610.825.0282 E-mail: wmcmenamin@yahoo.com

Philippians

Philippians is a social organization for seniors from our area. It offers entertainment, refreshments, trips, informational programs, and opportunities for service projects for our parish and the wider community. The group meets on the second Thursday of each month (except July and August) at 11 a.m. in the school hall.

Contact: Joan Meade 610.828.6641, Ann Marlin 215.233.1527, or Harriet Decker 610.828.1268

Women's Guild

"Our Mission: To promote friendship and support among women of St. Philip Neri Parish."

Parishioners who gather to share ideas and experiences to enhance the fundraising capacity of St. Philip's Parish, to meet some of the needs of the Parish School and to explore ways to provide an arena where each parishioner can feel welcome and to participate in affairs as they are able. A desire for "camaraderie" is an important base as each new project forms.

Contact: Mrs. Lindsay Glace Sirolli 610-331-6110 E-mail: LGlace@gmail.com
